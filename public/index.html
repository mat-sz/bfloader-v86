Demo:

After running, type in your Brainfuck program using your keyboard (use arrow keys to move the cursor) and press F5 to execute it (or Ctrl + F5 to start step by step execution). The output will be shown in the lower half of your screen.

To stop the execution hold Ctrl + C. While debugging press F5 to continue the program from the current step until the end.
